People Shot by Police by Race and Gender

This bar chart uses data from the Washington Post on people who were shot and killed by policy officers in 2015. It illustrates the two demographic factors that are most skewed when it comes to police shootings: race and gender. The limitation of this graph is that it is not scaled to the demographic breakdown in the population, so it does not clearly illustrate how police shootings disproportionately affect males from minority races. Incorporating supplementary demographic information on the population would improve the graph significantly. Data source
