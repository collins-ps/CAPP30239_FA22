Temperature

Mean and maximum temperature have been steadily rising across the years.

While this might seem gradual, seemingly small shifts are significant when it comes to temperature.

The distribution of mean and max temperatures recorded in a month gives a sense of the magnitude of the shift.

While there are year-to-year and month-to-month fluctuations, each decade is warmer than the last.

Every month has been getting warmer across the years.

Purple is cooler, orange is warmer.

Rainfall

Annual rainfall is significantly influenced by climate events such as El Nino and La Nina.

Data needs to be normalized to analyze changes in underlying weather pattern.

Purple is dry, yellow is wet.
